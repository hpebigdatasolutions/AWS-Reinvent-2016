Overview

Overall, the conference was huge - number of attendees, sessions, new feature announcements, impressions, and so on. Obviously, there was a lot of koolaid, designed to pull one into the AWS fold. But at the same time it was impressive to see the decidedly educational and cost saving angle of many of the sessions. This is an attempt to consolidate notes and impressions (including external links) from the conference.
